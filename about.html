About LTS

LTS was founded in 2022. Our shop was built from a love of tacos 🌮🌮🌮. At the Little Taco Shop, we take pride in using only the freshest ingredients sourced from local suppliers. Each taco is carefully crafted with love and attention to detail, ensuring a burst of flavor in every bite. Whether you prefer classic options like carne asada or adventurous combinations with unique toppings, our menu has something to please every palate.

Taco Trivia

Which Mexican city is widely regarded as the birthplace of the modern taco?

Mexico City is considered the birthplace of the modern taco. Tacos have a rich history in Mexican cuisine, but it was in Mexico City where they evolved and gained popularity. The city's vibrant street food culture and diverse culinary influences have contributed to the wide variety of taco fillings and flavors available today. From the iconic street tacos al pastor to the mouthwatering carnitas, Mexico City offers a taco experience like no other.
